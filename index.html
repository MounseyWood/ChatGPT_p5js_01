Cloth Simulation

This panel controls the cloth simulation.

• Damping reduces velocity each frame (like friction).
• Iterations is how many times we relax constraints per frame.
• Gravity & Weight pull the cloth downward.
• Wind gently pushes the cloth horizontally.
• Stretch, Shear, Bending control how the cloth resists different kinds of deformation.

Click or tap on the cloth (to the right) to drag points around!
